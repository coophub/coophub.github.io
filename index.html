About


We help students and employers involved in the university coop process
by being a middle man between companies and universities. Coop hub is a
universal hub that allows students to view coop job offers on a single portal.

It streamlines the hiring process by allowing employers to advertise positions
to multiple schools at once. In this way, they do not have to familiarize themselves
multiple complicated portals while experiencing no loss in exposure of the job offering,
allowing the employer to select the most fitting students and spend less time doing so.

Students have easy access to job offerings that meet their needs, and can apply
to coop jobs across Canada in one click. This platform includes a fully customizable
resume creator and eliminates the stress that can come with the hiring process.
